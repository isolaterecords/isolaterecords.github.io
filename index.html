Our Mission

We at Isolate Records are a nonprofit pushing student-created art and raising awareness and money for marginalized causes using whatever proceeds we make. This creates an outlet not only to benefit the community but also for students to express themselves, and earn volunteer hours while doing so. We were motivated by our interests in development and art.
